Details are given on the annual change in body weight of Leisler's bat (\emph{Nyctalus leisleri}) showing a different seasonal pattern between males and females. These differences are evident especially in September, when males are engaged to form and defend harems.

Here are provided data on sexual size dimorphism in the forearm length and change in the size of testicles.
